7. PERSONAL HEALTH PLAN

My Health Priorities:​

  1. Improve sleep schedule to get at least 8 hours per night
  2. Reduce screen time and spend more time outdoors
  3. Increase physical activity through regular walking
  4. Improve nutrition by reducing processed foods
  5. Practice consistent sun protection

Action Steps for Each Priority:​

1. Sleep Improvement​

  • Set a 10:00 PM phone alarm to start bedtime routine
  • Put away all electronics by 10:30 PM
  • Be in bed with lights off by 10:30 PM

2. Screen Time Management​

  • Set screen time limits on phone (2 hours for social media/games)
  • Take 10-minute screen breaks every hour
  • Plan outdoor activities for after school

3. Physical Activity​

  • Schedule walks 4 times per week
  • Find a walking buddy for motivation
  • Create walking playlists to make it more enjoyable

4. Nutrition​

  • Pack healthy snacks for school
  • Limit fast food to once every two weeks
  • Drink water instead of sugary drinks

5. Sun Protection​

  • Apply sunscreen every morning
  • Wear a hat when outside for long periods
  • Seek shade during peak sun hours
